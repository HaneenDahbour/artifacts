Section 4 · Comparison
Same cluster. Different intelligence.
What changes when llm-d sits between your LB and vLLM.
⊗ Without llm-d
Kubernetes routes blindly — no KV cache knowledge
All pods treated equally regardless of cache state
Cold pod = full context rebuild from scratch
Prefill and decode compete for same GPU memory
TTFT spikes unpredictably under load
Every team invents their own workaround
Vendor lock-in risk — no open governance
✓ With llm-d
EPP routes to warmest KV cache every time
Cache-aware pod selection in <1ms
Cache hit = no rebuild — 40% TTFT reduction
Prefill and decode split across dedicated pods
Latency stays stable under high load
CNCF Sandbox — open governance, community-owned
Any model · any accelerator · any cloud
